Dr. L. S. Jayashree is a Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore, with over 25 years of teaching experience and 20+ years of research leadership. She received her B.E. in Electronics & Communication Engineering (1995, GCT Coimbatore), M.E. in Computer Science & Engineering (1997, GCT Coimbatore), and Ph.D. in Computer Science & Engineering (2009, Anna University).

Her core research interests span the Internet of Things (IoT), Wearable Assistive Systems, Edge Analytics, Mobile Networks, and Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare. She played a pivotal role in establishing the DST-supported Centre of Excellence for Assistive Technology and Rehabilitation Engineering Solutions (PSG CARES) in 2019, where she serves as Professor-in-Charge and Coordinator, guiding translational engineering designs from proof-of-concept (TRL 3) to market-ready clinical validation (TRL 9).

She actively participates in the innovation ecosystem, serving as the Vice President of the PSG Institution's Innovation Cell (IIC) since 2020 and playing an instrumental role in drafting the institutional Start-up Policy. She is a Senior IEEE Member, a Fellow of the Institution of Engineers (India), and a member of the ACM India Expanding Regions Tech Policy Committee.

Technology acquires its true value only when it reaches the people who need it most. My research therefore extends beyond scientific discovery to encompass clinical validation, technology translation, commercialization, and ecosystem development, ensuring that digital innovations improve accessibility, independence, and quality of life for underserved populations.

— Dr. L.S. Jayashree, Research Philosophy

DST BDTD & SEED Schemes

Gait Watch

Wearable Fall Prediction System (TRL 9)

Gait Watch is a clinically validated, smart-wearable system designed for continuous monitoring of gait profiles, posture stability, and real-time fall state classification. The system uses biomedical IMU sensors communicating with edge nodes, mapping gait dynamics to predict fall events in neurological and geriatric care contexts.

Key Achievements & Specs:

  • Clinical Trial Validation: Tested with multiple patient batches at PSG IMSR and Neuro Foundation.
  • Technology Transfer: Licensed to Agna Inc. in 2020 (5-year validation).
  • Hospital Deployment: Active deployment for orthopedic gait camp evaluations.
  • IP Status: Protected under Granted Indian Patent: 201941053600.
Clinical Trials Report Launch Press Release

DiaSole

Foot Pressure Monitoring Insole

DiaSole is a high-resolution pressure-mapping shoe insole designed for diabetic neuropathy diagnosis and general podiatry evaluations. By monitoring continuous peak-pressure points, DiaSole alerts patients to ulceration risks and helps therapists inspect the balance mechanics of diabetic patients.

Technical & Commercial details:

  • Sensing Module: Flex-printed capacitive/resistive sensor array.
  • Mobile App: Provides dynamic gait cycle charts and historical pressure heatmaps.
  • Integration: Supports customization of corrective medical footwear.

DST-TIDE Funding Scheme

WING Kit

Wearable Intelligent Navigation Guidance Kit

Developed under the DST-TIDE scheme, the WING (Wearable Intelligent Navigation Guidance) Kit is a compact, sensor-rich companion system designed to aid elderly dementia patients. It mitigates caregiver distress by predicting fall status, establishing geo-fences, and offering tactile vibration/voice direction cues to navigate wandering seniors safely home.

Functional Elements:

  • GPS & IMU Integration: Fall detection with immediate geographical positioning tags.
  • Audio & Haptic Cues: Dynamic navigation routing cues configured via a companion app.
  • Caregiver Portal: Real-time MQTT notification pipeline sending status messages.
DST SEED Project Output

Memory Aide

Cognitive Companion for Alzheimer's Care

Memory Aide consists of a smart tablet-like controller and wearable bands that work in tandem to assist cognitively impaired patients. It schedules prescription alerts, triggers activity reminders, and captures response metrics to evaluate behavioral patterns in dementia.

Clinical Impact:

  • Adherence Enhancement: Improved clinical drug adherence in pilot groups by 42%.
  • Technology Licensing: Transferred to Agna Inc. in 2019 (5-year agreement).
  • Reduction of Caregiver Burden: Automated logging of daily checklist completions.
Preventive Cardiology

VitaLink

Anticoagulation Dosage Management App

VitaLink is a specialized mobile health platform developed in collaboration with cardiology departments. It solves the critical challenge of manual anticoagulation dosage calculation by providing a dual-access doctor-patient interface. Doctors prescribe dynamic dosages based on INR inputs, and patients track ingestion with automated feedback.

Features:

  • INR Logging & Tracking: Visual analytics showing blood coagulation stability trends.
  • Prescription Engine: Fast, validated communication protocol for cardiologists to update dosages.
  • Emergency Alerts: Immediate caregiver warnings when abnormal INR levels are registered.
Clinical Diagnostic Tool

CaRes Fitwatch

6MWT Cardio Endurance Monitor

CaRes Fitwatch automates the 6-Minute Walk Test (6MWT) which is widely used in cardio-pulmonary rehabilitation. By tracking acceleration patterns and vital metrics during the walk, it computes precise cardio-endurance scores, removing manual count errors and enhancing clinical accuracy.

Technical Assets:

  • Validated Algorithms: High correlation coefficient with manual standard metrics.
  • Ethics Committee Approval: Tested ethically on heart failure rehab patients at PSG IMSR.

Active Research (Ongoing)

BalancePoseAI

Virtual Balance Rehab Assistant

BalancePoseAI is an ongoing rehabilitation project. It utilizes basic device webcams to execute real-time human skeleton tracking (MediaPipe engine). The system tracks older adults practicing gait-balance exercises at home, offering auditory biofeedback to prevent over-extension and tracking performance metrics long-term.

Key Specifications:

  • Zero Hardware: Operates entirely within standard web browsers without expensive sensors.
  • Bio-Kinematic Validation: Real-time joint angle computations matched to clinical goniometers.

Myora

Speech & Handwriting EMR Automation

Myora is an intelligent data-entry assistant for clinical environments. By running local Whisper-based speech models and offline digital-pen handwriting classifiers, Myora translates doctor-patient conversations and handwritten medical scripts directly into structured Electronic Medical Records (EMR).

Core Modules:

  • Medical Speech-to-Text: Fine-tuned on multi-lingual Indian accents.
  • Offline Script Parsing: Recognizes standard medical shorthand symbols.
